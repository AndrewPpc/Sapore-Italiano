Focaccia

Indulge in our classic Italian focaccia, a culinary delight from Liguria. Savor the airy crumb and crisp crust kissed with olive oil. Choose from traditional sea salt or aromatic rosemary toppings to elevate your experience.

Ingredients:

  • Bread flour (500g)
  • Salt (10g)
  • Active dry yeast (7g)
  • Lukewarm water (350ml)
  • Extra-virgin olive oil (60ml), plus more for drizzling
